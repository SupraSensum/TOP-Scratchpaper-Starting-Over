Peanut Butter & Jam Flapjacks

Description

Peanut Butter & Jam Flapjacks are a sweet, chewy treat combining rolled oats, golden syrup, and rich peanut butter, topped with a generous swirl of fruity jam. Baked until golden and slightly crisp at the edges, they offer a nostalgic nod to the classic sandwich combo in a wholesome bar format.

Perfect as a snack or lunchbox addition, these flapjacks strike a balance between indulgence and energy. The oats provide substance, while the peanut butter and jam create a familiar, comforting flavor that appeals to both kids and adults alike.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups rolled oats
  • 1 cup peanut butter
  • 1/2 cup jam
  • 1/4 cup honey

Steps

  1. Mix oats, peanut butter, and jam in a bowl until well combined.
  2. Add honey and mix until smooth.
  3. Press mixture into a lined baking dish.
  4. Refrigerate until set.
  5. Cut into bars and serve.
